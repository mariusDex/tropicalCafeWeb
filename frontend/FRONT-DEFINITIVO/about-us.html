OUR OBJECTIVE

We are an entity that tries to satisfy all our customers with the best coffees and infusions, so they can relax and fully savor the different types of flavors that we offer. Tropical coffee always attempts to find the best possible flavor combinations and we invite our customers to trust us, to have a rewarding experience thanks to the wide variety of products that we offer.

OUR STORY

Our beginnings return to a small dream among several friends of creating a small business in our hometown, Santa Clara. Step by step, our business expanded throughout the city thanks to the support of all our customers and we finally managed to open several establishments around the city, becoming the most important coffee chain in Santa Clara. Finally, after obtaining great repercussions in the sector, we have expanded throughout the county, being one of the leading coffee shops in the state of California
